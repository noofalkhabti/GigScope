🧠 Smart Hybrid Data Collection Model

GigScope uses a multi-layered estimation approach that does not depend on individual tracking. Instead, it combines real-world signals that indicate economic activity intensity.

  • 📍 Mobility signals from high-density zones such as delivery clustering points
  • 🔍 Search demand indicators reflecting service need over time
  • 📱 Aggregated platform activity indicators from non-personal usage patterns
  • 🧠 AI-based estimation models that convert signals into workforce visibility
  • 🔒 No personal data collection, no identities, and no direct worker tracking

Privacy & Data Ethics

🛡️

This dashboard is designed to demonstrate the GigScope concept using aggregated and anonymized indicators only. The model focuses on patterns, zones, and time windows rather than individuals.

  • No names, phone numbers, IDs, plate numbers, or device identifiers are collected.
  • Outputs are displayed as regional and temporal estimations only.
  • The system is designed around privacy-safe, non-personal economic intelligence.
